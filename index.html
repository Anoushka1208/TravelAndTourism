Varanasi

Escape to Varanasi's luxurious sanctuary, the Taj Ganges. This 5-star retreat nestled on 12 verdant acres offers a haven of modern comfort and timeless elegance. Unwind in spacious rooms with city or garden views, and experience the world-renowned Taj hospitality. Discover a perfect harmony between spiritual exploration and luxurious indulgence at the Taj Ganges.

Taj Ganges

Escape to Varanasi's luxurious oasis, the Taj Ganges. This 5-star retreat, nestled on verdant grounds, offers modern comfort, stunning views, and legendary Taj hospitality. Unwind, explore the holy city, and indulge in unforgettable experiences.

Kashmir

Embark on a journey to Kashmir, the "Paradise on Earth" nestled in the majestic Himalayas. Lush valleys cradle glistening lakes like Dal and Nageen, while snow-capped peaks pierce the sapphire skies. Experience a rich cultural tapestry, explore charming houseboats, and discover serenity amidst breathtaking landscapes. Kashmir awaits!

The Khyber Himalayan Resort & Spa

Paradise awaits at The Khyber Himalayan Resort & Spa. This luxurious retreat in Kashmir boasts stunning mountain views, world-class service, and a rejuvenating spa. Wake up to breathtaking panoramas, indulge in gourmet dining, and explore the slopes in style. The Khyber: luxury meets adventure in Kashmir.

Kolkata

Kolkata beckons! Dive into the energetic spirit of India's "City of Joy." Marvel at architectural marvels like the Victoria Memorial, wander through bustling markets like Chandni Chowk, and savor iconic Bengali cuisine. Immerse yourself in the city's artistic heritage, from museums brimming with treasures to vibrant street art. Kolkata promises an unforgettable cultural adventure.

ITC Royal Bengal

Unveiling Kolkata's regal stay, ITC Royal Bengal! This LEED Platinum certified masterpiece offers a blend of heritage and modern luxury. Indulge in spacious rooms, world-class cuisine from 7 restaurants, and rejuvenate at the renowned Kaya Kalp spa. Experience Kolkata's cultural tapestry in style.
